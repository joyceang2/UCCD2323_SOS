Strength Training Program

Build muscle, increase strength, and improve your overall fitness

Workout Overview

  • Duration 45 minutes
  • Difficulty Intermediate
  • Target Areas Full Body
Equipment Needed
  • Dumbbells
  • Exercise Mat
  • Resistance Bands
  • Water Bottle

Workout Routine

  • Arm circles (1 minute)
  • Body-weight squats (2 minutes)
  • Push-ups (1 minute)
  • Light stretching (6 minutes)

Circuit (3 sets, rest 1-2 minutes between sets)

  • Dumbbell squats (12 reps)
  • Dumbbell rows (12 reps)
  • Dumbbell chest press (12 reps)
  • Dumbbell lunges (10 reps each leg)
  • Dumbbell shoulder press (12 reps)
  • Plank hold (45 seconds)

  • Light stretching for major muscle groups
  • Deep breathing exercises

Training Tips

Form & Safety
  • Start with lighter weights to perfect form
  • Keep your core engaged throughout exercises
  • Breathe steadily during movements
Progress Tips
  • Gradually increase weight when exercises become easy
  • Track your workouts and progress
  • Rest adequately between workout days
