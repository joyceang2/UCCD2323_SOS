Nutrition Guide

Discover the fundamentals of balanced nutrition and learn how to fuel your body for optimal health and wellbeing.

Why Nutrition Matters

Proper nutrition is the foundation of good health. The food you eat provides the energy your body needs to function and affects how you feel today, tomorrow, and in the future.

Nutrition is about eating a healthy and balanced diet so your body gets the nutrients that it needs. Nutrients are substances in foods that our bodies need to function and grow. They include carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, minerals, and water.

At Healthy Living, we believe in science-based nutrition advice that helps you make informed choices about the foods you eat.

Understanding Macronutrients

Macronutrients are the nutrients your body needs in larger amounts to provide energy and support basic functions. Let's explore the three main macronutrients:

Proteins

The building blocks of your body's tissues, including muscles, bones, skin, and hair. Essential for growth, maintenance, and repair.

Sources:

  • Meat, poultry, and fish
  • Eggs and dairy products
  • Legumes and beans
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Soy products like tofu

Recommended Intake:

10-35% of daily calories

Carbohydrates

Your body's main source of energy, providing fuel for your brain, muscles, and other vital organs.

Sources:

  • Whole grains (brown rice, oats)
  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Legumes and beans
  • Starchy vegetables (potatoes)
  • Dairy products

Recommended Intake:

45-65% of daily calories

Fats

Essential for hormone production, cell membrane integrity, and absorption of fat-soluble vitamins.

Sources:

  • Avocados and olive oil
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Fatty fish (salmon, mackerel)
  • Eggs and full-fat dairy
  • Coconut and cocoa

Recommended Intake:

20-35% of daily calories

Healthy Recipes

Healthy recipes prioritize balanced nutrition through fresh, whole ingredients like vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats.

🥗 Herb Lemon Chicken Breast Salad

Prep 10 mins | Cook 15 mins

📝 Ingredients (2 servings)

  • Chicken breast 200g
  • Mixed salad greens 150g
  • Cherry tomatoes 10 pieces
  • Lemon juice 2 tbsp
  • Olive oil 1 tsp

🍳 Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon juice and black pepper for 10 mins
  2. Pan-fry chicken with minimal oil and slice
  3. Combine washed greens with chicken
  4. Dress with olive oil and remaining lemon juice
📊 Nutrition Facts (per serving)
Calories 280kcal
Protein 35g
Fat 8g
Carbs 6g

🍔 Japanese-style Tofu Burger Steak

Prep 15 mins | Cook 20 mins

📝 Ingredients (2 servings)

  • Firm tofu 300g
  • Ground chicken 150g
  • Minced onion 1/4 piece
  • Breadcrumbs 2 tbsp
  • Teriyaki sauce 1 tbsp

🍳 Instructions

  1. Press tofu to remove excess water
  2. Mix all ingredients and form into patties
  3. Pan-fry until golden brown
  4. Glaze with sauce and reduce
📊 Nutrition Facts (per serving)
Calories 320kcal
Protein 28g
Fat 12g
Carbs 18g

🥣 Berry Yogurt Oatmeal Jar

Prep 5 mins | No cooking

📝 Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Unsweetened yogurt 150g
  • Instant oats 30g
  • Mixed berries 50g
  • Chia seeds 1 tsp

🍳 Instructions

  1. Layer oats at jar bottom
  2. Alternate yogurt and berries
  3. Sprinkle chia seeds on top
  4. Chill 1 hour before serving
📊 Nutrition Facts (per serving)
Calories 250kcal
Protein 12g
Fat 5g
Carbs 35g
