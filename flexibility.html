Yoga & Flexibility Program

Enhance your flexibility, balance, and mental wellness through guided yoga practices

Session Overview

  • Duration 45 minutes
  • Level Beginner Friendly
  • Focus Areas Full Body & Mind
Required Items
  • Yoga Mat
  • Comfortable Clothing
  • Water Bottle
  • Small Towel

Yoga Session Flow

  • Seated meditation (3 minutes)
  • Deep breathing exercises (4 minutes)
  • Gentle neck and shoulder rolls (3 minutes)

Flow Sequence

  • Sun Salutation A (5 minutes)
  • Standing Poses (10 minutes)
    • Warrior I & II
    • Triangle Pose
    • Tree Pose
  • Seated Poses (10 minutes)
    • Forward Folds
    • Spinal Twists
    • Butterfly Pose
  • Gentle Back Bends (5 minutes)

  • Final relaxation in Savasana (3 minutes)
  • Closing meditation (2 minutes)

Practice Guidelines

Mindful Practice
  • Practice on an empty stomach
  • Focus on your breath
  • Move mindfully and slowly
Safety Tips
  • Listen to your body
  • Don't force any poses
  • Maintain steady breathing
