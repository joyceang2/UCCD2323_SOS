Why Relaxation is Crucial for Mental Health

Chronic stress can harm your body and mind. It increases the risk of heart disease, weakens your immune system, and contributes to mental health problems like anxiety and depression. Taking time each day to relax helps lower stress hormone levels and improves overall well-being.

Techniques like deep breathing, meditation, and mindful walking are simple yet powerful ways to reduce stress. Prioritizing relaxation is not a luxury — it’s a necessity for a healthy life.
